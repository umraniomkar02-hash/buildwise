ABOUT BUILDWISE

Smart Preliminary Construction Budget Planning

BuildWise is an academic interdisciplinary engineering project designed to estimate realistic Indian house construction budgets using market-based approximations.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Why BuildWise Exists

Problem Statement

Traditional construction budgeting often involves rough manual calculations, inconsistent assumptions, and poor visibility into actual project costs.

Our Solution

BuildWise provides a premium web-based budget estimation platform that combines engineering assumptions with approximate Indian market pricing.

Objective

To help users estimate realistic construction budgets within a practical accuracy band for preliminary planning.
