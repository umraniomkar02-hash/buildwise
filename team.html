Interdisciplinary Engineering Collaboration

BuildWise was developed as a collaborative academic innovation between Civil Engineering and Information Science Engineering students.

TEAM STRUCTURE

Department Contributions

💻 Information Science Engineering

Responsible for the software architecture and digital implementation of the BuildWise platform.


• Frontend web development

• Dashboard UI/UX design

• Interactive cost estimation engine

• Chart-based visualization

• Platform integration

🏗 Civil Engineering

Responsible for engineering logic and construction cost estimation methodology.

• Indian construction pricing assumptions

• Structural estimation logic

• Validation of engineering formulas

• Domain expertise

PROJECT WORKFLOW

Development Process

Step 1

Civil Engineering students identified practical construction cost components and engineering estimation assumptions.

Step 2

Information Science students designed and developed the premium BuildWise estimation platform.

Step 3

Both teams collaboratively refined pricing assumptions, estimation logic, and dashboard presentation.
