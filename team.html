PROJECT TEAM

BuildWise Project Team

BuildWise is an interdisciplinary project developed by students of Information Science Engineering and Civil Engineering at Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering. The project combines software engineering, construction cost estimation, and material analysis to provide an intelligent platform for residential construction budgeting and planning.

TEAM STRUCTURE

Department Contributions

💻 Information Science Engineering

Responsible for the software architecture and digital implementation of the BuildWise platform.


• Frontend Web Development

• Dashboard UI/UX Design

• Interactive Cost Estimation Engine

• Chart-Based Visualization

• Platform Integration & Testing

🏗 Civil Engineering

Responsible for engineering logic and construction cost estimation methodology.


• Material Quantity Assumptions

• Indian Construction Pricing Analysis

• Structural Estimation Logic

• Engineering Formula Validation

• Construction Domain Expertise

PROJECT WORKFLOW

Development Process

Step 1

Civil Engineering students identified practical construction cost components, material requirements, and engineering estimation assumptions.

Step 2

Information Science Engineering students designed, developed, and integrated the BuildWise estimation platform using web technologies.

Step 3

Both teams collaboratively refined pricing assumptions, estimation logic, user experience, and dashboard presentation to improve accuracy and usability.
